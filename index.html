About Me

Bonjour! I am a computer science student specializing in software engineering at the University of Toronto. I constantly dream of building applications using new technologies, hoping to be apart of the future.

What got me started in programming was having a keen interest in building a website to post homework due dates for fellow class mates in middle school. Thus, I had started learning front-end web development with HTML5 and CSS3 coming out at the time. Once I actually started building websites, I chose to learn some back end programming with PHP and also enrol in all the computer science classes that my high school had. As years progressed, I have learned multiple languages from past classes, projects, or work terms.

I am always interested in improving on existing skills or developing new ones. However, I only do my best learning when I get my hands dirty and actually build something. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in giving an individual an opportunity to learn while also providing a final product.
