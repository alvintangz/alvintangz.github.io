Biography

Greetings! I am a computer science student specializing in software engineering at the University of Toronto, with a keen interest in web development. The thrill coming from building a virtual product that many may use, makes me want to continue on this path, and maybe discover new ones.

My interests in the field of computer science started when I built a website to post homework due dates for fellow class mates in middle school. This allowed me to learn about front-end web development, which was perfectly aligned at the time when major HTML5 and CSS3 standards were coming straight out of the minds of the W3C and into major browsers. From that period on, I chose to progress my knowledge, so I learned back-end development with PHP, and also enrolled in all the computer science classes that my high school could offered. As years progressed, I have learned multiple languages, frameworks and new methodologies from past classes, projects, or work terms.

I continuously strive to refine and expand my knowledge in the field of computer science. If you think I am a good match for an opportunity, I would be glad to hear about it.
